These are a few artists who inspire me

Devin Elle Kurtz

Devin Inspires me because of her imaginative landscapes. She uses many saturated colors to emphasize focal points in her design, which inspires my color palettes.

Crenerit

Crenerit has many simple, unique logos that use clean typefaces to highlight the product. The products seem modern with the combination of type and log graphics.

Emi

Emi inspires me because of her use of dark screens in her designs. Dark screen is hard to design with, but she uses color to make the screens stand out.
